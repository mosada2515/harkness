Understanding Made Easier

Harkness is a virtual office hours system to connect professors and students. We seek to make the process of learning more efficient and effective in large lecture classes. Professors can upload their course materials and provide students with a place to ask nuianced questions to virtual "Professors" anywhere, anytime, and receive responses in real time.

For Professors

Track student participation, manage discussions, and gain valuable insights into classroom dynamics.

For Students

Engage meaningfully in discussions, receive feedback on your participation, and develop critical thinking skills.
